About Me

My name is Rhea and my greatest accomplishment is being a mother of 4 beautiful kids. Madison is 15, Noah is 12, Logan is 10, and Kendall is 8. I am married to my best friend Ed, who I met in college. I grew up as a navy brat, so I lived in many different places. I was born in Portsmouth, Virginia, and moved to Honolulu, Hawaii. After Hawaii, I lived in Seattle, Washington and then Port Hueneme, California. Then in the 7th grade, I moved to Orlando, Florida where I went to high school and college. I moved to California to attend Loma Linda University and have been here since. By trade, I am a cytotechnologist. What is that you ask? Well, cyto is the study of cells. So, I microscopically look at cells, looking for abnormalities, specifically cancer cells. Due to my field fading, I have decided to take on a new career path, and that is why I am in this class.
